2017 Twin Cities Blind Audio Dart League Tournament

You are invited to join us at the 2017 Twin Cities Audio dart tournament February 17, 18 & 19.  All dart boards at this tournament are fully accessible.


Location:

The tournament will be held at the Crown Plaza and Suites 3401 American Blvd Bloomington, MN 55431

Room Reservations:

Room rates are $95.00 plus 14.275% tax for single/double occupancy per
night.  Suites are also available for $125 plus 14.275% tax for
single/double occupancy per night.  For overnight accommodations, call the
hotel directly at 952-854-9000.  Reservations should be made by January 20,
2017.  Inform hotel at time of reservation that you are part of the Twin
Cities Audio Dart Tournament.


Cost:

Registration is $40 per event or $100 for all events.  After February first,

all entry fees are $120.


Games:

All participants are required to wear a legal blindfold and furnish their
own darts.  All blindfolds and darts are subject to inspection by the
tournament committee.  Darts must be no heavier than 18 grams and must have
nylon tips.  The tournament will begin at 7:00 pm Friday night, and should
wrap up by approximately 2:00 pm on Sunday.  We will be not having any door
prizes this year, we may have raffles during the tournament on some nicer
items though.

Breakfast/Dinner:

The hotel offers a breakfast buffet each morning or get a grab and go with
coffee at their coffee shop.  Lunch and dinner may be purchased at the hotel
restaurant.

Food and drink restrictions:

Food and drink may be brought in to the banquet room (dart room) from the
restaurant, bar, coffee shop and vending machines only.

Shuttle Service:

The hotel provides complimentary shuttle service to and from the airport
and Mall of America.  The lite rail also stops across the street from the
hotel.
